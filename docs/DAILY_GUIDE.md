# How to use CoffeeTime every day (1 page)

Open the app on your tablet or phone and log in with your email +
the 6-digit code sent to you. You stay logged in on the same device.

## 🌅 Morning (2 minutes)
1. Open **Home**. Check the red **Low stock** tile — if it shows a number,
   tap it and see what needs restocking.
2. Milk or groceries delivered? Go to **Stock** → tap **In** next to the
   ingredient → enter quantity. (If it came from a regular vendor with a
   bill, use **Vendors → Purchases → New purchase** instead — that also
   tracks what you owe them.)

## ☕ During the day — billing
1. Tap **Billing** (or the green **New bill** button on Home).
2. Tap the category on top, then tap items — tap again to add one more.
3. On a phone, tap the bar at the bottom to open the bill.
4. Use **+ / −** to change quantities, enter a **discount** in ₹ if any,
   pick **CASH / UPI / CARD / MIXED**, then tap **Complete Sale**.
5. On the bill screen: **Print** for the thermal printer, **WhatsApp** to
   send the bill to the customer, **Start next bill** to continue.

Made a wrong bill? You can see every bill under **Sales** — nothing is
ever lost.

## 🌙 Evening (3 minutes)
1. **Stock** → tap **Out** for things used up or wasted today (milk spoiled,
   etc.). Reason "Used in kitchen" or "Wastage".
2. Paid rent, salary, or the electricity bill? **Expenses → + Expense**.
3. Paid a vendor? **Vendors → Purchases** → tap **Record payment** on
   their bill.
4. Glance at **Home**: today's total, number of bills, top 5 sellers.

## 📅 Weekly / monthly
- **Reports**: pick a date range, then see **Sales** by day, **Items**
  (what sells best), **Payments** (cash vs UPI vs card), and **P&L**
  (sales − purchases − expenses). Tap **CSV** to download any table for
  your accountant.
- **Vendors**: the yellow card shows total money you owe; each vendor
  shows their own due, oldest bills first.

## ✏️ Changing the menu
**Menu** → tap **+ Item** (or **Bulk add** to paste many at once, one per
line like `Cappuccino, 120`). Prices include GST — what you type is what
the customer pays. Use the eye button to hide an item instead of deleting
it — old bills stay correct forever.

## 🆘 If something looks wrong
- "Could not load…" message → check the internet and pull down to refresh.
- Wrong stock number? **Stock → In/Out** with a note, so there's a record
  of the correction.
- Locked out? Just log in again with your email — a fresh code is sent
  every time.
